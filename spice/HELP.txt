To read bsp file:
-----------------
from jplephem import spk
k = spk.SPK.open('de440s_2030.bsp')
print(k)
# or: print(k.comments())
k.close()

To Excerpt bsp file:
--------------------
python3 -m jplephem excerpt 2022/1/1 2026/1/1 jup380s.bsp jup380s_2225.bsp
